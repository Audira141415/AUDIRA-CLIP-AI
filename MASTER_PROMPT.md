# AUDIRA CLIP AI

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

Build a production-ready enterprise SaaS platform called "Audira Clip AI".

Audira Clip AI is an AI-powered video clipping and content repurposing platform capable of:

* Uploading long-form videos
* AI scene detection
* AI highlight detection
* AI viral moment detection
* AI subtitle generation
* AI speaker recognition
* AI face tracking
* AI reframing
* AI social media export
* Team collaboration
* AI Copilot Assistant
* Cloud rendering
* Multi-tenant architecture

The platform must support:

* Web
* Tablet
* Mobile
* Ultrawide displays
* 4K dashboards
